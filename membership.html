Hello Prospective Members!

Welcome to BSSA’s webpage to learn more about how to become a member of the association. Our site gives you a glimpse into BSSA’s values, professional achievements, and individual personalities.

BSSA is built off of each member’s commitment and investments into the organization. We are dedicated to assisting you in your professional growth and helping you transform challenges into opportunities. As a member, you will be provided with abundant resources, mentorships, networks, and workshops that will further your success. With our exclusive faculty and alumni network, you will be able to explore all fields in the division of biology and more.

There is no membership fee to be part of the association. However, this year, BSSA is highlighting membership with a new feature, membership points! Members with the most accumulated points at the end of each quarter will be recognized and rewarded. As a note, membership is carried over from year to year, BUT membership points are reset from quarter to quarter.

Membership Points:

It is also important to note that in order to be considered as a potential officer for next year’s board, you must fulfill the following qualifications.

  • Qualifications:
  • -Minimum of 30 membership points (for the academic year)
  • -Required to be part of one BSSA committee

The inclusive community is surrounded by talented and driven individuals who support each other in the university, the professional realm, and beyond. We highly encourage you to push your present potential and lay the foundation for your future with BSSA.
