Pie-a-Professor Fundraiser and Charity

Coming back soon in 2019

Each year, BSSA holds the Pie-a-Professor Fundraiser. One of our most popular events every year, Pie-a-Prof is a unique and exciting opportunity to pie your favorite professors while raising money for charity and supporting your favorite UCSD biology org. For the past two years, we have funded over a thousand meals with Feeding San Diego, a local hunger-relief non-profit organization.

Check out our previous Pieings!


2018: Dr. Reuther — Funded 816 meals through Feeding San Diego


2017: Dr. Johnson — Funded 550 meals through Feeding San Diego


2016: Dr. Reuther — 20% of proceeds to the San Diego Brain Injury Foundation
